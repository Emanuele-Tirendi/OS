The Vienna Opening

There are two Versions of the Vienna-Opening namely:

  • The Vienna Gambit
  • The Vienne Game

About the Vienna Gambit:

The Vienna Gambit starts with the moves: e4, e5 followed by the moves kc3, kf6

Typically white plays the next move pawn to f4 attacking the black pawn on e5.

Black can respond with a series of possible moves. Possible lines are:

  • Nxe4, taking the pawn and attacking the night on c3
  • exf4, capturing the pawn with another pawn and therefore accepting the gambit.
  • d6, defending the pawn in the centre
  • d5, attacking the pawn on e4 (which is considered the main line)
